This is the unique software for the unique company - Agency of Atmospheric Technologies ATTEX, whose business is fog, hail, frost suppression, protection against snow avalanches and so on. FlightMonitor is part of hardware-software complex which is developed together with RadugaTelecom. The complex is designed for dispatching aircrafts while development works and for data communication between boards and the base.

The program includes 'locations' editor, that are constituted by datum points, geographical map in azimuthal projection and set of radar maps. With using different locations the complex can be used at any point of the world.

The complex has been used in development works at Moscow, Rostov-na-Donu, Chelyabinsk regions of Russia, Kazakhstan and for science researching at Cuba.

Now we are working on new version of the program, which includes many new features.
