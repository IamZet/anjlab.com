AnjLab.Scada is part of hardware-software complex, developed in collaboration with ZAO RadugaTelecom. The complex is designed for using in intrusion alarm systems, resources (electricity, heat powers, etc) technological counting, dispatching and telemetry purposes on power engineering plants, housing and communal services, transporting and industry enterprises. The system allows to setup hardware configuration, transport channels, queue discipline, describe layer of calculating parameters (including arbitrary complex calculating algorithms) and design user interface. Software part of the complex has modular architecture:

  • Central service and database.
  • Configuration designer.
  • Client program.
  • Data analyzer.
  • Web-interface to access live data.

The architecture allows to customize the system to fit specific requirements and easily includes supporting for new hardware devices (data exchange protocols). For data exchanging can be used Ethernet (TCP\IP), RS-232\RS-485, including transmitting data over radio channel.

Now the complex is deployed at 'City electricity network' municipal enterprise at Megdurechensk city, Kemerovo region, Russia.
