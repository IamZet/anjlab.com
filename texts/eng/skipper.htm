The system of integrated management of financial instruments. It is our venture project which we are developing in collaboration with Triumph-Capital investment company. Basic system functionality is securities accounting, dealerships supporting, estimation of business effectiveness. In comparing to existing 'heavy' systems on Russian market, Skipper will have the following features:

  • Ability to count all widespread financial instruments and basic financial operations: buy-sell, REPO deals, bail deals. Ability to present data in various sections.
  • Directivity of the system to wide range of players on financial market - private investors, investment companies, investment brokers, banks.
  • Robustness and simplicity in installing, configuring and usage. 'Thin client' architecture which allows to use the system over the internet.
  • Integration with third-party systems: front-end and back-end systems, specialized internet-services, supporting for SOA.
  • Ability to use the system as 'out of the box' desktop software as well as software as service (SaaS).
